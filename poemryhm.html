The Rhyme

I do not want this to rhyme.
This is not rhyme time.
Do not make this a rhyme,
Or copy me like a mime.
Time for things that don’t rhyme,
Because this is not my favorite time.
Mime, rhyme, time,
Do not eat this like a sour lime.
If this tastes like a bad time,
This ends with a similar word, chime.
If you may leave,
This is what to believe.
I like the look of willow,
And to lie down on my pillow.
